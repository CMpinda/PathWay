Information About Coaching

With the Path Way, we offer you a chance to improve your skill. The training sessions will be suited for everyones needs and ability.

The Prices to vary, depending on what type of coaching you are looking for.

To have a 1-2-1 session: £30 Per Hour

To get involved in one of our coaching sessions, just contact us via email or phone number. You can find this information on our Contact Us page.
